The Acquisition System

Five steps.
Systematic. Repeatable.

01
Foundation
Technical Infrastructure

Deliverability is the foundation. We build a sending infrastructure designed to keep your emails in the inbox permanently. Every domain and inbox is warmed, monitored and rotated on a schedule. No burned domains, no downtime, no periods of silence. Before a single email goes out, the system is already running.

02
Data
Lead Data Your Competitors Are Not Using

Most companies pull from Apollo, ZoomInfo or PeopleDataLabs and send to the same lists as everyone else. We built our own data platform, XenoLeads, to access leads that have not been hit by every agency in your space. We waterfall verify using MillionVerifier, Enrichley and Scrubby, recovering 20 to 30% more valid contacts that standard verification misses.

03
Offer
Offer Engineering

Cold traffic is not warm traffic. We build your offer specifically for cold email: lead with value, deliver something tangible, and only transition to the service once trust is established. The offer is tested across multiple angles before we scale anything. Most agencies send one offer forever. We treat it as a variable.

04
Testing
Campaign Launch and Live Testing

We launch multiple campaigns simultaneously, testing different angles, ICPs and messaging variants. Every variable is tracked. What performs gets scaled. What does not gets cut within the first two weeks. This is not spray and pray. It is a structured testing process with clear decision criteria at each stage. The system gets sharper every week it runs.

05
Revenue
Sales Process Integration

A booked call is not a closed deal. We audit your current sales process, identify where prospects are dropping off, and build the infrastructure to fix it: automated reminders, pre-call sequences, follow-up flows and sales assets designed for cold-sourced leads. Show up rates and close rates are metrics we optimise too.
