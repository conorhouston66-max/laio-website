How it works Results Getting started
How is this different from a standard cold email agency?+
Most agencies run 1-2 campaigns per client and optimise by instinct. We run 15+ in parallel, treating every campaign as a structured test. Within 3-4 weeks you have real conversion data across angles, audiences, and CTAs. We double down on what's working. The result is faster learning and compounding pipeline growth instead of a slow drip of guesses.
What tools do we need in place?+
Not much. You'll need a CRM (or we can help you pick one) and access to a domain for sending infrastructure. We handle everything else: inbox setup, warmup, list building via Apollo, deliverability monitoring, and sequencing through our sending stack. You don't need to own any outbound tools.
How quickly will we see results?+
Realistically, first replies come in week 2-3. Meaningful data on what's converting typically shows up at the end of week 3. Because we're running so many tests simultaneously, we compress the learning timeline that would normally take 3-4 months with a traditional agency into 3-4 weeks.
Do we need to commit long-term?+
We offer an initial sprint engagement to prove the system works before any long-term commitment. Once we have winning campaigns and a functioning outbound system, most clients choose to continue on an ongoing basis. You're never locked in before the work proves itself.
Can you work with our existing sales team?+
Yes, and we prefer it. We build the outbound infrastructure and fill the top of the funnel. Your salespeople handle the conversations and close. We can also build CRM workflows and handoff sequences so nothing falls through the cracks when a reply comes in.
Who is this best suited for?+
B2B companies or agencies that have a clear offer but inconsistent pipeline. Typically founders doing all the selling themselves, or sales teams that have tried cold email but can't get it to convert. If you have a product people want but struggle to get in front of them systematically, this is built for you.
